Support the Clowser House Fund

The Citizens’ Committee to Preserve the Clowser House
and the Clowser House Foundation need your help!

This early house on the frontier of Virginia was the home of Johann Heinrich Clausser and his wife Anna Marie. In a 1764 indian attack Heinrich and his eldest sons were killed, his wife and daughters taken. Only his youngest child Henry was left behind. But the family survived, the daughters miraculously returned home and the family continued to live in the house well into the 1900's. Read the story: Clowser House

Efforts to save the Clowser House for future generations are nearing a successful conclusion, but funds are necessary to meet current and future expenses.

Help save this early 1750's frontier house today!
